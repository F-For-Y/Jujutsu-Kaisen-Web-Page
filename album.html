TVテレビアニメ「呪術廻戦じゅじゅつかいせん」オリジナル・サウンドトラック:
Yuji Itadori is a boy with tremendous physical strength, though he lives a completely ordinary high school life. One day, to save a classmate who has been attacked by curses, he eats the finger of Ryomen Sukuna, taking the curse into his own soul. From then on, he shares one body with Ryomen Sukuna. Guided by the most powerful of sorcerers, Satoru Gojo, Itadori is admitted to Tokyo Jujutsu High School, an organization that fights the curses... and thus begins the heroic tale of a boy who became a curse to exorcise a curse, a life from which he could never turn back.

Film: Jujutsu Kaisen 0

劇げき場じょう版ばん「呪術廻戦じゅじゅつかいせん 0ゼロ」オリジナル・サウンドトラック:
Yuuta Okkotsu is haunted. Ever since his childhood friend Rika died in a traffic accident, her ghost has stuck with him. But her spirit does not appear as the sweet girl Yuuta once knew. Instead, she manifests as a monstrous and powerful entity who fiercely protects him. Unable to control Rika's violent behavior, Yuuta is helpless to stop the bloodshed that follows from her brutal vengeance. Yet his apprehender, the master sorcerer Satoru Gojou, has different plans for him: he will join Jujutsu High School and learn to control Rika in order to help people. Now a first-year at this school, Yuuta starts to learn Jujutsu arts and combat malignant beings.


Episode: season2

「呪術廻戦 懐玉・玉折」オリジナル・サウンドトラック:
The storyline set in Tokyo's Shibuya district, where a large-scale battle unfolds between sorcerers and curses. This pivotal arc begins with antagonists orchestrating a chaotic attack on Halloween, leading to the sealing of the powerful sorcerer Satoru Gojo, a crucial turning point in the battle. The protagonist Yuji Itadori, along with his allies, faces intense and morally challenging battles against various curses and cursed users. Key characters, including Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, play significant roles and undergo substantial character development. The aftermath of this arc significantly impacts the characters and sets a new course for the jujutsu world, highlighting the series' intricate storytelling and character dynamics.
